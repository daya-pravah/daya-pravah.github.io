Our Story

Daya Pravah is a story of compassion, hard work, and vision. Established in honor of Saroj Chauhan, a farmer from a small district in Uttar Pradesh, our journey began with her dedication to farming and community service. At the age of 65, she continues to cultivate the land and donate surplus produce locally. Saroj ji also actively employs women farmers to promote women empowerment, further enriching her legacy of service and community development.

Carrying her legacy forward, her family has expanded the vision to supply NGOs with high-quality dry rations directly from their own 50-acre farmland. With crops like rice, mustard, wheat, jaggery, and sugarcane, we ensure that every grain reflects our commitment to quality and care.

Our Vision

To be the trusted partner for NGOs in their fight against hunger by delivering fresh, affordable dry rations at affordable prices.
