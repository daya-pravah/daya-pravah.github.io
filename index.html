Vision

To build a future where no one goes hungry and every community thrives through access to quality produce, fostering lasting change.

Mission

We aim to empower local communities by delivering fresh, quality produce from our farms and uplifting women, bridging the gap between surplus and scarcity.

Our Story

Inspired by the remarkable legacy of Saroj Chauhan — a farmer with over 40 years of experience and an unwavering commitment to farming and philanthropy—Daya Pravah continues her vision to nourish communities and uplift lives. Her dedication to supporting the local community by distributing farm produce to those in need laid the foundation for our mission.

Building on her passion, our family has taken this initiative to a larger scale by delivering affordable, high-quality dry rations directly to NGOs, instead of selling them in traditional markets. This approach not only ensures that essential resources reach those who need them most but also empowers the farmers and women who are an integral part of our process.

By involving local women and farmers, we’ve created a sustainable ecosystem that supports livelihoods, sustains rural economies, and prioritizes quality. Every grain and product we deliver contributes to a greater purpose: nourishing people, strengthening communities, and fostering a sense of shared responsibility.

Our mission is simple yet profound—to serve social support organizations who are at the forefront of community service, by ensuring that our produce reaches the people who depend on it most. Together, we can create lasting change and bring Saroj Chauhan’s vision to life.

Let’s join hands to fight hunger and create opportunities.
