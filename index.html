Our Mission

At Daya Pravah, we are redefining the way dry rations reach those in need. Inspired by the legacy of Saroj Chauhan, a farmer with over 40 years of experience, we deliver premium-quality, farm-fresh dry rations directly to NGOs at affordable prices.


Our mission is simple: To nourish communities and empower NGOs with the quality they deserve.


  • Directly sourced from our own 50-acre farmland.
  • Guaranteed quality, freshness, and affordability.
  • Dedicated to timely deliveries for your cause.

Together, we can fight hunger and uplift communities.
