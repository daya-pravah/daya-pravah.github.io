Vision

Our vision is to create a hunger-free society by bringing quality, farm-fresh products to every doorstep in need.

Mission

We strive to empower local communities through sustainable farming and equitable opportunities, bridging the gap between surplus and scarcity.

Our Story

Inspired by the legacy of Saroj Chauhan and her unwavering dedication to farming and philanthropy, Daya Pravah carries forward her vision to nourish communities and empower NGOs.

Our family has expanded her mission, ensuring that every grain and every product we deliver helps sustain local economies and uplift families in rural communities.

By involving local women and farmers in our processes, we’ve fostered a sustainable ecosystem—one that supports livelihoods, emphasizes quality, and truly cares about the people it serves.

"Nurturing communities with compassion, forging a path to a brighter tomorrow."
